Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions about the usecase of the product.

  • How to create a classroom?

    Just click to plus("+") sign in the top bar.You'll find create class option there.Write classname and description.Then press create,it is that simple

  • How to join a classroom?

    Just click to plus("+") sign in the top bar.You'll find Join class option there.Use class code provided to you and you will automatically get enroll in respective class

  • How to join a live class?

    Check the time-slot box of the class, in there you'll find the option to 'Join Class', enter the class-code as well as your name.

  • What is a live-board?

    Live-board is a teaching utility wherein a teacher can write something on their board and all the students tuned into the board will be able to follow it real-time.
